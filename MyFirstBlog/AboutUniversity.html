About Our University

    The Tarlac Agricultural University (TAU) formerly named Tarlac College of Agriculture is the best University in the province of Tarlac. Led by Dr. Max P. Guillermo the President that has many capabilities to improve the Tarlac Agricultural University that was located in Malacampa, Camiling, Tarlac, The Carabao in the University is the symbol of resilience even through the ages, synonymous with action and sustained accomplishments.

    This institution was established in 1944 as Camiling Boys/Girls High School. Tarlac Agricultural University has many offers courses to all upcoming freshmen and Transferees like the course Bachelor of Science in Information Technology and also it major programs includes agriculture, Biosystems engineering, food technology, veterinary medicine, forestry and other agriculture related programs. TAU being environment-friendly institutions embraces the utilization of the organic fertilizer in the ensuring better production and implementation of researches on its adopted areas. This universities it is helpful to all student who study the agriculture like the production of organic fertilizer using beneficial microorganisms so that they have many programs of this university.

    Tarlac Agricultural University achieved excellence in undertaking the full range of its functions as a state university or college that include research, and extension manifested through teaching effectiveness, research competence, active community service, and efficient management of the resource. The world rank of Tarlac Agricultural University is 141 among 11264 schools worldwide during 2021. Tarlac Agricultural University is only one public university that is ranked among the world’s best universities by the Times Higher Education (THE) it is among the five Philippines Universities ranked in 2021 it is the Ateneo De Manila, De La Salle University, Mapua University, Tarlac Agricultural University and University of Santo Tomas. Tarlac Agricultural University has continuously improved it ranking and has achieved level 4 statuses in the last CHED leveling exercise. It also joined most of the internationalization programs of CHED, particularly with Canadian Universities and has inked partnership agreement with 7 Canadian colleges and universities. In an environment of academic excellence, Tarlac Agriculture University harnesses, develops, and catalyzes the conversion of student’s full potentials and capabilities into becoming responsible and competent professionals in agriculture and allied disciplines.
